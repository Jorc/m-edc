m edc - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Overview

m edc is an application focused on providing haptic feedback experiences. We highly value your privacy and adopt a principle of minimal data collection.

Data Collection

m edc does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Usage behavior data
  • Any other personal data

In-App Purchases

When you purchase the Pro version, the purchase process and payment handling are entirely managed through Apple's App Store and StoreKit framework:

  • Purchase transactions are processed by Apple; we do not directly access or store your payment information
  • Apple is responsible for managing your transaction records, receipts, and account security
  • All purchase confirmations, refunds, and subscription management are completed through your Apple ID account
  • We only receive purchase status verification information provided by Apple (Pro unlock status) and do not store any transaction details

For Apple's data processing, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All your settings and custom data (such as saved haptic sequences) are stored only locally on your device, and we cannot access this data:

  • Pro unlock status is stored on your device through Apple's secure verification mechanism
  • All haptic sequences, theme preferences, and custom settings are saved locally
  • No data is uploaded to any server

Third-Party Services

m edc does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Network Connection

Except for in-app purchase verification, m edc does not require a network connection to use all features normally.

Children's Privacy

m edc does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose children's personal information.
